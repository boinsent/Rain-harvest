How does it work?

The Rainwater Harvesting System (RWHS) is essentially a mechanism that enables the collection and storage of rainwater within tanks or reservoirs for a variety of applications. It utilizes a range of devices and components to realize the desired functionality. Our system incorporates purposeful sensors with distinct roles. Initially, a sensor positioned on the roof of the structure detects the presence of raindrops. Subsequently, when raindrops are detected, a pipe connected from the roof to the reservoir is activated, allowing for the controlled transfer of rainwater. Our system benefits from the implementation of Artificial Intelligence (AI), which plays a pivotal role in enhancing its overall functionality. See more information here.
